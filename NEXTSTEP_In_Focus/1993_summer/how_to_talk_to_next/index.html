HOW TO TALK TO NEXT

You can reach NeXT several different ways. We look forward to hearing from you!

To reach NeXT in North America

Information, product registration, and order status

To get NeXT literature or find the location of your nearest NeXT sales office or reseller, call  1-800-TRY-NeXT.
To order or find out about support products, or to register new NeXT products and one-year limited warranties, call 1-800-848-NeXT and press option 4, fax (415) 363-5188, or write us at:
Customer Support
NeXT Computer, Inc.
900 Chesapeake Drive
Redwood City, CA 94063 U.S.A.
For product order status, call 1-800-848-NeXT and press option 5 or fax 1-800-228-NeXT.

Technical support

For information on many technical topics, use NeXTanswers, an automated information retrieval system. To get files by fax using a touch-tone phone, call
(415) 780-3990. To get files by e-mail, send mail with the subject "HELP" to NeXTanswers@next.com. To get files by anonymous ftp, connect to
ftp.next.com. NeXTanswers is a free service.
For technical support through the System Support or Developer Hotline, call 1-800-848-NeXT and press option 4, or send e-mail to Ask_NeXT@next.com. (There's a charge for this support.)
To comment on the quality of support you receive, send e-mail to the address Sysadmin_Comments@next.com or to Developer_Comments@next.com. You can also fax comments to (415) 363-5188.
